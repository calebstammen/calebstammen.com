Chapter 03

A process built for reality.

The process is simple. The discipline is not. Good systems come from staying honest about constraints while still pushing for meaningful change.

01

Diagnose

Clarify the outcome, surface the constraints, and choose the smallest lever that changes the system.

02

Design

Align architecture, data, governance, and human workflow so adoption feels natural instead of forced.

03

Deliver

Ship in increments, measure what changed, and add guardrails that help the system hold up over time.
