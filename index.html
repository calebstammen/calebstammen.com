About

I started in technology the unglamorous way.

I saved for a laptop, learned to code, fixed systems, supported users, and eventually owned the infrastructure those systems depended on. That path still shapes how I build software. I care whether a system works in the real world, whether people can trust it, whether it fails safely, and whether it removes friction from someone's day.

Today, I lead AI initiatives and build software at Garmann Miller, run Stammen Technology as a solo-owned software studio, and build products such as SuitFlow and Atlas.
